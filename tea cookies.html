Almond Tea Cookies

Ingredients

3/4 cup 3/4 cups 3 cups flour

6 tablespoons of 3/4 cups/ 1 1/2 sticks 1 1/2 cups/ 3 sticks softened butter

2 tablepoons 1/4 cup 1/2 cup sugar

2 1/2 tablespoons 5 tablespoons 10 tablespoons semolina

Pinch of salt

2 teaspoons 4 teaspoons 8 teaspoons almond extract

3 1/2 tablepoons 7 tablespoons 14 tablespoons chopped almonds

Sliced or crushed almonds for topping

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325 fahrenheit, Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment.

Add the flour, sugar and semolina in a medium bowl, mix lightly. Add butter, mix thoroughly with electric mixer until it will hold together when pressed

Mix in almond extract and chopped almonds

Refrigerate for 10 minutes

Then, roll into about 20 ball on baking sheets and flatten with your hand.

Push sliced almonds on the top of the cookies

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cookies start to brown around the edges

Cool completely on the baking sheets

Stir Fry

Ingredients

1/2 lb 1 lb 2 lb Chicken thighs

3 6 12 oz any type of noodles

1/4 1/2 1 onion

1 1/2 3 6 cloves of garlic

1 1/2 3 6 tablespoon of grated ginger

1/2 1 2 large bell pepper or 2 1/2 5 10 small bell pepers

1 2 4 cups spinach

1/4 1/2 1 cup cabbage

1/4 1/2 1 cup shredded carrots

2 1/2 5 10 or so tablespoons of soy sauce

1 1/2 3 6 splashes of white vinegar

1/2 1 2 or so tablespoon of saracha (change acording to spice preference)

1/2 1 2 tablepoon seseme oil

add black peper, garlic powder and ginger powder to taste if needed

To garnish 1/4 cup small sliced scallons

1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions

Pour 1 tablespoon of oil on a pan, put on high heat and when pan is hot add chicken thighs until all pink is gone

Then remove chicken from pan

Boil noodles acording to instructions

Add onion, garlic, ginger with oil in a pan

Once fragrant and onions until soft, add all vegtables to pan with a bit of water and cover and steam

After 8 minutes, add noodles, chicken and sauce (soy sauce, white vinegar,saracha and seseme oil) and stir on medium heat until mixed well, add extra soy if needed

Once done add garnishes (black peper, garlic powder and ginger powder,1/4 cup small sliced scallons ,1 tablespoon sesame seeds)

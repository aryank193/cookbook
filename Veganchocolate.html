Vegan Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

1 2 Tablespoons apple cider vinegar

1/2 1 cup Soy or other vegan milk

3/4 1 1/2 cups sugar

1 cup 2 cups all-purpose flour

6 tablespoons 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1/2 1 tablespoon baking powder

1 teaspoon 2 teaspoons baking soda

1/2 teaspoon 1 teaspoon sea salt

1/4 cup 1/2 cup + 1 teaspoons 2 teaspoons avocado oil

1 teaspoon 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

1/2 cup 1 cup boiling water + 1/2 teaspoons 1 teaspoon instant expresso powder pluss a little bit

Instructions

Grease one two 9" cake pan pans

Heat oven to 350 fahrenheit

In a small bowl, add apple cider vinegar and the milk together still to combine and curdle

In a seperate bowl whisk together sugar, flour,cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a large bowl until thoroughly combined.

Prepare instant coffee

Add milk mixture,oil, vanilla and the coffee water.

Beat with an electric whisk on medium soeed fir about 2 minutes until everything is combined

Scrape down the sides of the bowl and stir one more time to make sure everything is incorporated

Pour the batter into the cakepan pans

Bake for 35 minutes

remove and cool

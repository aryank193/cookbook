Chocolate Mousse Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

10 20 oreos

2 tablespoons 1/4 cup brown sugar

3 1/2 tablespoons 7 tablespoons melted butter

Filling

16 oz 32 oz Softened, cream cheese

2 oz 4 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips

1/2 tablespoon 1 tablepoon cocoa powder

1/2 cup 1 cup white sugar

1 teaspoons 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

2 4 eggs

3/4 cups 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

2 tablespoons 1/4 cup powdered sugar

8 oz 16 oz chocolate chips

1 cups 2 cups heavy cream, hot

Instructions

Preheat oven to 300F

Crush cookies and mix in a bowl with melted butter and brown sugar, until coarse like wet sand

Pour crumbs into a springform pan and press into an even bottom

Then refrigerate

Add cream cheese,melted chocolate, cocoa powder, and sugar to a large bowl and whisk together until incorporated

add vanilla and eggs, and continue to whisk until mixture is smooth and glossy

In a searate bowl, combine heavy cream and powdered sugar and whisk until soft speaks

adding small amounts at a time fold in whipped cream into the cream cheese batter, until incorporated

Once combined, pour the batter over prepared cookie crust inside the springform pan, then smooth out the top

Lightly tap the pan to get rid of trapped air

Place a sheet of aluminum foil and fold the foil up the sides of the outside of the pan

Then place the wrapped pan in a sheet pan and fill with bout 1 inch of hot water

Bake for 60-70 minutes, check every 15-20 and add more hot water as necessary

Once out of the oven, run a small sharp knife around the outside of the cheesecake, once cool completely

Once cooled prepare ganache by combining chocolate chips with hot cream

Stir until chocolate is completely melted and smooth

Pour ganache evenly over the cooled cheesecake

Refrigerate the ganache covered cake for 30 minutes before cutting and serving

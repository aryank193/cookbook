Banana Bread

Ingredients

1/2 cup / 1 sticks melted unsalted butter

3/4 cup white sugar

2 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup sour cream

2 medium bananas mashed

choclate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F in a 9X5 inch loaf pan

In a large bowl, stir melted butter and sugar

add eggs and vanilla, mix well

combine flour,baking soda and salt

incoporate into butter mixture until smooth

Fold in sour cream,bananas, and chocolate chips

bake at 350 for 60 minutes until center comes out clean

cool completly and remove from pan and slice

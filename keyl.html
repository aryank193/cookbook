Key Lime Pie Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust

3/4 cup 1 1/2 cups 3 cups graham cracker crumbs

3 tablespoons 6 tablespoons 12 tablespoons melted unsalted butter

Filling

12 ounces 24 ounces 48 ounces cream cheese, softened

6 tbsp 3/4 cup 1 1/2 cups white sugar

1/2 tablespoon 1 tablespoon 2 tablespoon cornstarch

1 1/2 3 6 eggs

1/2 tablespoon 1 tablespoon 2 tablespoon grated lime zest

1/3 cup 2/3 cup 1 1/3 cup key lime juice

3/4 tablespoon 1 1/2 tablespoons 3 tablespoons key lime zest

1/4 teaspoon 1/2 teaspoon 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

pinch teaspoon 1/8 teaspoon 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Crush 1 1/2 packs of graham crackers, and then combine with butter melted

After, press into tart pan or springform pan, then refrigerate.

Preheat at 300 degrees fahrenheit

In a large bowl, beat with an electric mixer, cream cheese, sugar, lime zest, and cornstarch until smooth.

Beat eggs one at a time, mixing until smooth

Add key lime juice,vanilla and salt on low

Finish mixing by hand, do not over mix

Pour batter into prepared crust

Bake for 55 to 65 minutes, or until set. To minimize cracking, place a shallow pan half full of hot water on lower racknto minimize cracking

Once done, turn oven off and let cheesecake sit in the oven for 30 minutes with the door cracked oven.

After, 30 minutes remove from oven. Refrigerate cake overnight, and up to three days

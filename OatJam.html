Oat Thumbprint Cookies

Ingredients

1/2 cup 1 cup 2 cups pecans

1/2 cup 1 cup 2 cup rolled oats

1/2 cup 1 cup 2 cup whole wheat pastry flour

1/4 cup 1/2 cup 1 cup avocado oil/baking oil

1/4 cup 1/2 cup 1 cup pure maple syrup

1/8 teaspoon 1/4 teaspoon 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt

about 3 tablespoons about 6 tablespoons about 12 tablespoons rasberry jam/ preferabed jam

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit

Grind Oats and Pecans finely in a food processor

add to a bowl with whole wheat pastry flour

Then add maple syrup,oil and salt

mix with rubber spatulas until fully incorporated

roll walnut sized ball and put on to tray

push a finger lightly into center of dough balls

take small spoon of jam and put into center of cookie

careful to not over flow with jam

bake for 16 - 20 minutes until sides are firm and golden brown

Tiramisu

Ingredients

3/4 cup 1 1/2 cups 3 cups cold coffee/ for instant 3/4 teaspoons 1 1/2 teaspoons 3 teaspoons expreso powder

3 6 12 egg Yolks

2 tablepoons 1/4 cup 1/2 cup sugar

8 16 32 oz Macarpone cheese

1/4 1/2 1 TBSP Vanilla extract

7-9 15-18 30-36 LadyFinger cookes

Chunk Dark Chocolate,grated

Optional: Coco powder to dust the top

Instructions

Make instant coffee and chill in the fridge

In a medium bowl, beat the egg yolks and sugar until light and fluffy

Fold in the Mascarpone and mix pghtly

Keep the mixture in the refrigerator for 30 minutes

After 30 minutes, pour chilled coffee in another bowl and add vanilla to it

Now spread a thin layer of Mascarpone mixture in serving dish

Sprinkle some grated chocolate

Dip the ladyfingers one by one in coffee and lay them on the layer of mascarpone

Sprinkle some grated chocolate

Spread a layer of Mascarpone

Sprinkle some grated chocolate

Repeat the whole process if you want another layer

Once finished with layers dust the top with coco powder

Then,Chill for at least 8 hours prefeably over night and serve

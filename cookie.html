Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Serving: about 24 cookies about 48 cookies

1/2 cup / 1 stick 1 cup / 2 sticks softened unsalted butter

a bit less than 1/2 cup 1 cup white sugar

a bit less than 1/2 cup 1 cup brown sugar

1 egg 2 eggs

1/2 1 teaspoon vanilla

1 1/4 cup 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1/2 1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 1 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 1/2 teaspoon salt

any type of chocolate ~~ chopping up a chocolate bar is highly recomended

Instructions

Using a handmixer cream together butter with white & brown sugar

Once butter and sugar are incorperated well,

mix in eggs and vanilla extract just until incorporated

In a new bowl mix all dry ingredents together

Once mixed incorporate dry ingedents into wet...

mix with handmixer until about half the dry ingredents are incorporated

Then chop the chocolate bar and include the chocolate into the dough with a spatula

Refrigerate for at least 2 hours

Preheat oven to 350F

Once chilled, scoop the dough into golf ball sized balls on a parchment lined tray

line dough balls evenly spaced so they do not bake into eachother

bake for 10-12 minutes until the bottom is lightly golden brown

Let cool for about 5 minutes and ENJOY!
